Office Account

How to create an office account.

Quickly learn how create an office account through these simple steps
1 Go to Administrator panel then click on the office accounts option pill, Then Choose create Office account, from the options provided in order to load the Registration form.
2 Select or create a Service unit Account i.e. Ministry, Organisation, or any other business firm under which you want your office account to be created.
3 Select or create a Branch Account under which you want your office account to be created.
4 Select or create a Department Account under which you want your office account to be created.
5 Now finally create Office Account by filling in the form provided to you.

User Account

How to create or add user(Staff) to the system.

Quickly learn how create a user(Staff) account, or add a user to the system through these simple steps
1 Go to Administrator panel then click on the User accounts option pill.
2 Select Register Staff Member from the options provided to lead you to the User creation / or staff registration form.
3 You shall then be prompted to Select Service unit, Branch, Department, and then Office Account under which you want to register the user.
Note: If you are not yet aware of how to create an office account, then follow the steps in the section above this on How to create an Office Account
4 Now finally add user or create User Account by filling in the form provided to you.

Reports or Documents

How to generate Reports or Documents.

Quickly learn how to generate reports and(or) documents through these simple steps
1 By default reports and Documents are generated basing on the default settings of {{ request.session.app }} Application set by its developers. In case you want to review the current settings on reports and documents, or you want to alter the default or current settings, then:
Choose and click on the General Settings option pill from the Administrator panel. Otherwise, you may skip this step in case you are Ok with the current settings.
2 Choose and click on the Reports & Documents Option pill from the Administrator panel, Then Choose either Reports or Documents.
3 You must then Choose or select Service unit for which or under which the report or document is to be generated.
4 In case you dont want to generate report for the entire Service unit, then You are required to Select up to Branch, or up to Department, or up to Office (basing on your choice) under or for which you want to generate the Report or Document.
Note: If you are not yet aware of how to create an office account, Department, Branch or service unit, then review the first section of this Quick guide on How to create an Office Account
5 Select time basis onto which report or document is to be generated i.e. Daily, weekly, Monthly, or Annual.
6 Finally In case of a report, Select the report type you want to generate i.e.Summarized, Detailed, Comparison, Analytical/statistical or Custom Report , Then you are set to generate your report.
